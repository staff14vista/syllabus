Lesson Plan - Prompt Engineering With AI

SL NO Menu/Module Session Detailed Sub-Topics
1 Introduction to Artificial Intelligence & Prompting 1.1 What is AI and how it works (basic concept, ChatGPT, Copilot, Gemini, etc.)
1.2 Understanding Prompts: What they are, why they matter
1.3 Structure of a Good Prompt (Role + Context + Task + Format)
1.4 Hands-on Practice: Writing first prompts (creative writing, summarization, idea generation)
2 Prompt Engineering for Better Results 2.1 Refining prompts: Using constraints, examples, tone, and persona
2.2 Iterative prompting: How to improve AI responses
2.3 Practical exercise: Compare "bad" vs "good" prompts
2.4 Using AI for decision-making & brainstorming
3 AI for Daily Tasks & Personal Productivity 3.1 Using AI for Email drafting, calendar, task management
3.2 Automating repetitive tasks (e.g. note summarization, translation, writing replies)
3.3 Using AI for learning and research assistance
4 AI for Reports, Writing, and Presentations 4.1 Using AI to create structured reports and outlines
4.2 Generating business, academic, or project reports
4.3 Creating presentations using AI (e.g. ChatGPT + PowerPoint/Canva, Gamma)
4.4 Practical Lab: Create a full report and presentation using AI
5 Ethical Use 5.1 Responsible & ethical AI use (bias, privacy, copyright)
5.2 Overview of AI trends and careers
5.3 Final Project: Apply AI to a real-life problem (e.g., automate a daily task, design a presentation)
